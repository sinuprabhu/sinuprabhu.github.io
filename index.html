Overview

Welcome to west Bangalore's tallest landmark - Sobha Rajvilas. The project is designed to give a new mode to luxury. Located in Rajaji Nagar, Bangalore, the project has artfully crafted 3 and 4 BHK apartments. The 2.70 acres is a culmination of world-class amenities right from recreation to medical support and much more. The model consists of 2 towers which reside on 25 floors and 160 units. The exceptionally well architecture provides all facilities within the units, but you can find coziness outside your residential units as well. For that reason, Sobha Rajvilas is built with large outdoor areas which have a room for plenty of luxuries. Apart from the indoor game zones, it redirects your path to a more smarter way of life at the modern clubhouse. Living at Rajvilas means you don’t have to cross the gates for spending your weekends. As the homes have got exclusive jogging tracks, well-equipped gym and lavish swimming pools, life isn’t boring within the gates. Since the project is located in a strategically important location, finding transportation, medical and educational institutions and facilities is not a difficult task as well. In fact, all services are right within your reach, including metro services and hospitals. Cut down the long hours of searching for your workplace or roaming for the shopping centers at Sobha Rajvilas. Live a marvelous life just in the right place!
